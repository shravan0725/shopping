Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your browser that help us remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality, security, and basic operations like shopping cart management.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site, allowing us to improve content and services.

Marketing Cookies: Allow us to deliver personalized advertising and track marketing campaign effectiveness.

Your Cookie Choices

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can disable non-essential cookies, though this may limit site functionality. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they're required for site operation.

Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party partners (analytics, payment processors) may set their own cookies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

Data Retention

Cookies are typically retained for up to 24 months, though this varies by cookie type. You can clear cookies anytime through your browser settings.

Contact

For questions about our cookie usage, contact privacy@luxeshop.com.
