Returns & Exchanges

Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer hassle-free returns and exchanges within 60 days of purchase.

Return Window: 60 days from delivery for full refund or exchange. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Return Process

1 Initiate Return

Contact our support team or use your account dashboard to start a return. Provide your order number and reason.

2 Get Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label. For orders over $250, return shipping is completely free.

3 Ship Your Item

Pack your item securely in original packaging. Use our provided label and drop off at any shipping location.

4 We Process Your Return

Upon receipt, we'll inspect and process your refund within 5-7 business days.

What Can Be Returned?

  • ✓ Unused items in original packaging and condition
  • ✓ Items within 60 days of delivery
  • ✓ Defective or damaged items (anytime)
  • ✓ Wrong items shipped to you

Non-Returnable Items

Custom orders, final sale items, and clearance products cannot be returned. However, defects are always covered.

Damaged or Defective? Contact us immediately at support@luxeshop.com. We'll arrange a replacement or refund right away at no cost to you.

Refund Details

  • ✓ Original payment method within 5-7 business days
  • ✓ Original shipping cost non-refundable
  • ✓ Free return shipping on orders over $250
  • ✓ Exchanges ship immediately at no extra cost
