Frequently Asked Questions

What is LuxeShop? ▼
LuxeShop is a premium online marketplace curating luxury goods and exclusive products from around the world, delivering them directly to your door with exceptional service.
How long does shipping take? ▼
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, Express 2-3 days, and Overnight delivery is available for next-business-day arrivals. See our Shipping page for more details.
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn and in original condition. Return shipping is free on all orders.
How do I track my order? ▼
After your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order from your account dashboard anytime.
Do you offer international shipping? ▼
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International orders take 7-14 business days and include full customs clearance and tracking.
Is my personal information secure? ▼
We use industry-leading SSL encryption and follow strict data protection standards. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
