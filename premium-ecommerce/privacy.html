Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

LuxeShop ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Account preferences and purchase history

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Browser and device information
  • IP address and location data
  • Cookies and tracking technologies
  • Browsing behavior and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Improve customer service and website experience
  • Send marketing communications (with consent)
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your personal information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information. Contact privacy@luxeshop.com to exercise these rights.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before providing information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Parents can contact us to remove such information.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@luxeshop.com or +1 (800) LUXE-SHOP
